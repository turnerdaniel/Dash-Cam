Post-Accident Checklist

In case of an accident, give us the number of a person you would like to notify by text:

  • Stop the car and turn off your engine.
  • Turn on your hazard lights to warn other drivers.
  • Check for injuries to yourself and passengers.
  • Attempt to stay calm - it's normal to be shaken after an accident.

Call Emergency Services if:

  • Anyone is injured.
  • The other driver leaves the scene without providing insurance details.
  • You think the other driver has no insurance or is under the influence of drink or drugs.
  • The road is blocked.

What Do I Need to do?

  • Share your name and address with everyone involved in the accident.
  • Swap insurance details with the other drivers.
  • Get the contact information of any witnesses.

What Information do I Collect?

  • Make, model, colour and number plate of all vehicles involved.
  • Time and date of the crash.
  • The driving conditions.
  • Damage caused - take photos as evidence.
